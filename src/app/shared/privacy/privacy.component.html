Introduction

EOXS respects each individual’s right to personal privacy. We will collect and use information through our Web site only in the ways disclosed in this statement. This statement applies solely to information collected at EOXS’s Web sites.


Part I. Information Collection

EOXS collects information through our Web site at several points. We collect this information via voluntary forms and do not collect any information on visitors involuntarily. To receive our products and services, contact information is mandatory so we may respond do your request. Other information we request is optional.


Part II. Information Usage

The information collected by EOXS will be used for contacting you or subscribing you to our e-newsletter. Users who provide their information to the e-newsletter will receive emails from us periodically. Out of respect for the privacy of our users we present the option to not receive these types of communications. The information we collect will be used to create customer profiles based on browsing or purchasing history. We will supplement information collected at our Web site with data from other sources such as credit history to authorize purchases. We will not share data with any third parties. We offer links to other Web sites. Please note: When you click on links to other Web sites, we encourage you to read their privacy policies. Their standards may differ from ours.
