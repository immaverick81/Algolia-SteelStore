A customer-centric steel distributor committed to industry-best service. The Steel Store is the service leader for all types of steel supply throughout North America with facilities located in Canada, and the United States.

TSS (The Steel Store) works closely with our customers to ensure the most cost-effective material is being supplied. We understand the demands of the industry & strive to help our customers to achieve the lowest cost.

The Steel Store provides a wide range of materials. Browse our most requested products like hot rolled, cold rolled and electro- galvanized steel materials for special projects and facility management. All featured products can be made to the customer's measurements and specifications.

At EOXS, you will be able to buy steel with confidence. We observed the lack of technology in steel procurement and decided to create a platform where buyer and seller have unfettered access to each other. Our primary focus at EOXS is to ensure customer satisfaction. We are here to make your transactions easier. The products undergo diligent quality checks before it reaches the customer. We believe that the customer journey continues even after a sale and are here to ensure your business receives the best of steel products.

You will not be limited by a narrow supplier pool. Our vetted suppliers are just a click away. We provide a technology driven, trustworthy platform that will simply help you do business better. We believe that the right processes can change the way businesses function. EOXS is here to enable that.

The process behind steel procurement is in need of a change. Most importantly, it needs to be driven by technology that is built to strive for cost effectiveness on both sides of the marketplace. EOXS is an online steel marketplace where buyers and sellers have easy access to one another. Our customers will have real time access to inventory levels through our ERP system that tracks real time movement of stock. No more narrow supply lines or high lead times.
